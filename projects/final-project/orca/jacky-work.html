Diet

Orca's diets usually consist of:

  1. Herring and Schooling Fish
  2. Seals and sharks
  3. Squid

Habitat

Orcas are special because they can be found in all the oceans of the world!
They are most abundant in waters like Antarctica, Norway, and Alaska.


Prey and Predators

Orcas are apex predators, so they are at the top of their food chain. They don't have many predators above them. They communicate with each other through clicks and squeals to catch their prey.
