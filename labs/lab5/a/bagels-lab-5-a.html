Homemade High-Protein Queso

Meeting protein goals certainly isn't easy; even more so when you actually want to enjoy what you're eating.
This is what led me to create a homemade queso recipe which is very high in protein while relatively low in calories.
Plus, who doesn't love queso? It's an incredibly versatile food which can be used in a variety of other dishes.


Ingredients:

  • Fat free shredded mozzarella
  • Fat free cottage cheese
  • Low moisture shredded chedder
  • Whipped cream cheese
  • Fat free milk
  • Whatever spices you like

Steps:

  1. Blend the whipped cream cheese and cottage cheese until smooth.
  2. Begin simmering the milk in a large pot.
  3. Slowly introduce the blended mixture into the milk once the milk starts lightly steaming.
  4. Then, while stirring constantly, very slowly add in both types of shredded cheese until you've reached your desired consistency.
  5. Finally, spice and mix before serving.
