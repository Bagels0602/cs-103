Habitat

  • The Snow petrel live in low sea-surface height and close to the southern side of the Antarctic Circumpolar Current.

Predators

  • Snow Petral are preyed among by Skuas. Their eggs and chicks are constantly in danger from them.

Fun Facts

  • Can fly up to 40 km/hr.
  • One of three birds that breed only in Antarctica.
  • Last remaining member of the Pagodroma genus.
  • Can produce oil within their stomach.
