Diet

  • Elephant Seal diets usually consist of:
    Fish
    Squids
    Rays
    Sharks

Habitat

  1. Northern elephant seals are found in the eastern and central North Pacific Ocean. They typically breed in offshore islands of California or Baja California in Mexico.

Prey and Predators

  • Elephant seals prey upon fish, squid and various other forms of medium-sized aquatic life.
  • Elephant seals, being one of the top ocean predators, are largely preyed upon by Orcas and Great white sharks.

Fun Facts

They spend 80% of their life in the ocean.

They can hold their breath for 100 minutes :0.
