The Big 4 Lifts

A pictoral example of the 4 main lifts in strength based weightlifting:
Deadlift for quads, hip flexors, and lower back. Bench for chest, triceps, and front delts. Shoulder press for side delts and upper back. Squat for quad and hamstrings.
